## Style Prompt

Warm editorial product-system visual for a private desktop library tool. The frame should feel like a polished app case-study still: paper-warm background, deep teal product identity, muted amber and coral accents, careful typography, and compact operational UI details. The subject is practical home-library cataloging, not marketing hype.

## Colors

- `#F4F0E8` page background
- `#FFFDF8` primary surface
- `#123F3A` deep teal brand/accent
- `#7FB3AA` secondary teal
- `#D7A861` warm amber
- `#C96D54` coral status accent
- `#1D2521` primary text
- `#59635F` secondary text

## Typography

- Headline: `Inter`, system sans-serif, 800 weight
- Body/UI: `Inter`, system sans-serif, 500-700 weight

## Motion

- Calm product walkthrough motion: short upward fades, subtle step pulses, slow background drift.
- Do not use neon, generic purple-blue gradients, bokeh blobs, or decorative card stacks.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use dark cyber UI.
- Do not use oversized generic hero text disconnected from the workflow.
- Do not make the flow look like a crypto or analytics dashboard.
- Do not add fake logos for external book sources.
